4. Euclidean Distance between two points

p1x = int(input('Enter x coord of 1st point: '))
p1y = int(input('Enter y coord of 1st point: '))
p2x = int(input('Enter x coord of 2nd point: '))
p2y = int(input('Enter y coord of 2nd point: '))
distance = ((p2x - p1x)**2 + (p2y - p1y)**2)**0.5
print('The distance between the points is:', round(distance, 2))
Points: (1,2), (4,6) → 5.0

6. Heads and Legs Problem

total_heads = int(input("Enter the number of heads: "))
total_legs = int(input("Enter the number of legs: "))
if total_legs < total_heads:
    print("Invalid input!")
else:
    dogs = (total_legs - (2 * total_heads)) / 2
    chickens = total_heads - dogs
    print("Number of dogs in farm are:", dogs)
    print("Number of chickens in farm are:", chickens)
Heads: 10, Legs: 28 → Dogs: 4.0, Chickens: 6.0

8. Find Nth term of Arithmetic Series

first_term = int(input('Enter 1st term: '))
second_term = int(input('Enter 2nd term: '))
n = int(input('Enter the value of n: '))
d = second_term - first_term
an = first_term + (n - 1) * d
print(an)
First: 3, Second: 6, n: 5 → 15

10. Milk Tank to Glass Calculation

import math
h_t = float(input('Enter the Height: '))
b_t = float(input('Enter the Breadth: '))
l_t = float(input('Enter the Length: '))
h_g = float(input('The Height of glass: '))
r_g = float(input('Radius of the glass: '))
vol_tank = h_t * b_t * l_t
vol_glass = 3.14 * r_g * r_g * h_g
print('No of glasses:', math.floor(vol_tank / vol_glass))
Tank: 10x19x10, Glass: height 5, radius 2 → no of glasses: 30
